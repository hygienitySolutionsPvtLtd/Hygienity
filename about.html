Currently in R&D | Pilots in Progress

Building preventive hygiene infrastructure from the material layer upward.

Hygienity Solutions is a startup focused on using nanotechnology to rethink the upkeep of sanitation systems. The company is still in its R&D phase and is validating its preventive coating approach through ongoing pilots and field observation.

Vision

To make hygiene infrastructure easier to maintain, more resource-efficient, and more reliable in everyday use.

Problem Statement

Sanitation systems are still maintained largely through reactive cleaning. That model can be expensive, water-intensive, labor-heavy, and inconsistent in quality. Hygienity is exploring whether preventive surface engineering can reduce that burden at the source.

Journey

01

Problem discovery

The team identified repeated pain points around stains, odor, water use, and manual cleaning intensity in sanitation infrastructure.

02

Material exploration

Nano coating concepts were developed to create a preventive layer that could change how treated surfaces behave in daily use.

03

Pilot validation

The company is currently learning from pilots across multiple environments to refine performance, durability, and implementation fit.

R&D Stage

Hygienity is currently in formulation refinement, field observation, and pilot validation. The company is not yet presenting full-scale commercial claims and is using active pilots to build an evidence base.

What Comes Next

The next phase is expected to focus on broadening validation, building partner relationships, and translating pilot learnings into scalable deployment and measurement frameworks.
