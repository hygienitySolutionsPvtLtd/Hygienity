Currently in R&D | Pilots in Progress

Pilot case studies built for learning, validation, and operational insight.

These examples describe ongoing pilots rather than finalized commercial deployments. Observations are shared conservatively and are based on real-world testing environments still under evaluation.

Government Pilot

Municipal sanitation block

Context

High-footfall public toilet infrastructure with visible wear and routine cleaning pressure.

Problem

Persistent stains, odor buildup, and high labor intensity in daily maintenance.

Intervention

Selected surfaces were treated with Hygienity’s preventive coating during an active pilot cycle.

Observations

Based on pilot observations, coated surfaces appeared easier to clean and showed lower visible residue retention across monitored intervals.

Status

Pilot ongoing.

Corporate Pilot

Office washroom environment

Context

A premium office setting where hygiene presentation and maintenance efficiency are both high priorities.

Problem

Frequent cleaning was required to maintain a polished appearance in high-use washrooms.

Intervention

Coating applied to selected fixtures as part of a controlled preventive maintenance evaluation.

Observations

Based on pilot observations, facility teams reported encouraging signs of easier upkeep and improved visual consistency between service cycles.

Status

Pilot ongoing.

Residential Pilot

New-build residential bathrooms

Context

Bathrooms in residential units where ease of maintenance and appearance retention are valuable to occupants.

Problem

Hard water marks and fixture staining contribute to repeated manual cleaning effort.

Intervention

Preventive coating introduced during the pilot period on selected bathroom surfaces.

Observations

Based on pilot observations, users indicated that treated surfaces were easier to wipe down and retained a cleaner appearance for longer periods.

Status

Pilot ongoing.
