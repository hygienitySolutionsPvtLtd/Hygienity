What Is Hygienity

A preventive hygiene layer for high-use sanitation assets.

Instead of relying only on repeated scrubbing and harsh chemical interventions, Hygienity applies a functional nano coating designed to make sanitation surfaces less prone to staining, residue retention, microbial buildup, and odor persistence.

Surface Behavior

Hydrophobic performance

Engineered to support easier runoff of water and residue so surfaces stay visually cleaner between maintenance cycles.

Hygiene Support

Anti-microbial intent

Designed as an added hygiene-supporting layer that may help reduce bacterial growth patterns based on pilot observations.

Operations

Preventive maintenance model

Shifts facility teams toward lower-frequency heavy cleaning while helping preserve infrastructure quality and user experience.
