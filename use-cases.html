Currently in R&D | Pilots in Progress

A preventive coating platform for multiple sanitation environments.

Hygienity is being evaluated across settings where hygiene quality directly affects operating cost, user trust, and maintenance burden. Each use case below frames the core problem, the proposed solution, and the early expected outcome.

Public Toilets

Problem

Heavy usage creates persistent stains, odor buildup, and water-heavy cleaning demand.

Solution

Apply a preventive nano coating to critical ceramic and washroom surfaces.

Outcome

Based on pilot observations, surfaces may remain easier to clean with reduced deep-clean effort.

Corporates

Problem

Facility teams need premium washroom presentation without escalating maintenance overhead.

Solution

Introduce preventive surface treatment in office restrooms and high-visibility hygiene zones.

Outcome

Cleaner-looking assets and more predictable maintenance routines may improve operational efficiency.

Homes

Problem

Households deal with frequent water marks, hard stains, and labor-intensive bathroom upkeep.

Solution

Apply the coating to bathroom fixtures where appearance retention matters most.

Outcome

Users may experience easier routine cleaning and better surface appearance based on pilot observations.

Construction

Problem

Developers and builders want long-term hygiene value beyond conventional fixture specification.

Solution

Integrate preventive coatings as a value-added layer during project delivery or handover.

Outcome

Could enhance asset differentiation while lowering downstream maintenance friction for operators and residents.

Transport

Problem

Buses, rail systems, and transit hubs require fast sanitation turnaround under constant usage pressure.

Solution

Treat washroom surfaces where downtime is limited and maintenance windows are narrow.

Outcome

May support easier cleaning, better passenger perception, and stronger hygiene consistency between service windows.
